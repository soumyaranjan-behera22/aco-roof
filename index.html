FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Have a question not answered here? Our team is ready to help you find the right solution for your home.

📞 Talk to an Expert
How much does a roof replacement cost in Dallas?
+
Roof replacement costs in Dallas typically range from $8,000–$20,000+ depending on roof size, pitch, material type, and complexity. A standard 2,000 sq ft home with architectural shingles averages $12,000–$15,000. We provide free, itemized estimates so you know exactly what you're paying for — no surprises.
Will my homeowner's insurance cover storm damage repairs?
+
Most homeowner's insurance policies cover storm damage including hail, wind, and lightning. ACO Roofing works directly with all major insurance companies. We'll perform a thorough inspection, document all damage, and work with your adjuster to ensure you receive the maximum claim value. Our insurance assistance is completely free.
How long does a roof replacement take?
+
Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1–2 days. A standard 2,000 sq ft home is typically done in a single day. Larger or more complex roofs may take 2–3 days. Commercial projects vary. We always work to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
What roofing materials do you offer?
+
We install a wide range of roofing materials including: architectural asphalt shingles (our most popular), premium designer shingles, metal roofing (standing seam and metal shingles), clay and concrete tile, flat/low-slope TPO and modified bitumen, and synthetic slate. We'll help you choose the best material for your climate, budget, and home style.
What warranty do you provide on your work?
+
ACO Roofing provides a lifetime workmanship warranty on all roof replacements and a 5-year workmanship warranty on repairs. Additionally, material warranties are passed directly to you — GAF Timberline shingles carry a lifetime limited manufacturer warranty, and most materials have 25–50 year coverage.
Do I need to be home during the roofing project?
+
You do not need to be home for most of the project. We just ask that someone is available briefly at the start so we can walk through the project plan together, and at the end for a final walkthrough. We send text/email updates throughout the day so you're always informed.
How do I know if I need a repair or a full replacement?
+
The decision depends on the age of your roof, extent of damage, and cost-effectiveness of repair vs replacement. As a general rule: roofs under 15 years old with isolated damage are good repair candidates. Roofs over 20 years old, or with more than 30% damage, often make more financial sense to replace. Our free inspection gives you an honest, unbiased recommendation.
Are you licensed and insured in Texas?
+
Yes. ACO Roofing & Gutters holds Texas Roofing Contractor License #TXRC-2009-4821 and carries $2 million in general liability insurance plus workers' compensation coverage. We can provide proof of insurance and licensing upon request. We strongly recommend always verifying your contractor's credentials before signing any contract.
